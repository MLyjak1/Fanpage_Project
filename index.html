Shakey Graves




Shakey Graves Bio

Shakey Graves is an eclectic artist that has a wide variety of sounds, and crosses genres frequently. He began as a solo artist with his self released debut album Roll the Bones. If you watch the videos, you will notice he plays primarily acoustic guitar and uses pedals for a kick drum and tambourine. He was signed by Dualtone Records and began forming a full band around his unique sound. He partnered with artist Esme Patterson for several duets; the most notable of which (and a personal favorite of mine) is Dearly Departed. The duo performed the song on the Late Show with David Letterman. He was awarded the "Best Emerging Artist" award in 2015 by the Americana Music Awards.



My Experience with Shakey Graves

I have personally seen Shakey Graves (AKA: Alejandro Rose-Garcia) live in concert once at The Masquerade in Atlanta, GA. His stage presence is everything you would expect it to be if you have watched his interviews and how he talks to others in his youtube recordings. He freely jokes with crowd and by the end of his set, you feel like he's one of your friends. Some of my favorite songs by him are: Roll the Bones, Kiss the Girl (Yes, from "The Little Mermaid,") and the original Dearly Departed He always looks like he is not only having a blast when he is singing, but he is truly putting his heart and soul into his music. His lyrics are asounding and heartfelt as well, and many of his tunes are catchy (I guarantee you will find yourself whistling the opening to Dearly Departed.)
